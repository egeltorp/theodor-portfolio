‚öôÔ∏è Derivative Rules

Sum / Difference
\((f \pm g)' = f' \pm g'\)
Constant Multiple
\((cf)' = c \cdot f'\)
Product Rule
\((fg)' = f'g + fg'\)
Quotient Rule
\(\left(\dfrac{f}{g}\right)' = \dfrac{f'g - fg'}{g^2}\)
Chain Rule
\((f(g(x)))' = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x)\)

Chain rule: differentiate outside, keep inside, multiply by derivative of inside.

üî¢ Factoring Methods

AC Method (for ax¬≤ + bx + c)
Find two numbers that multiply to ac and add to b, then split and group.
Difference of Squares
\(a^2 - b^2 = (a+b)(a-b)\)
Perfect Square
\(a^2 \pm 2ab + b^2 = (a \pm b)^2\)
Sum / Diff of Cubes
\(a^3 \pm b^3 = (a \pm b)(a^2 \mp ab + b^2)\)

Always check for common factors first before using other methods.

üîÑ Integration Rules

Fundamental Theorem
\(\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)\) where \(F' = f\)
Substitution (u-sub)
Let \(u = g(x)\), then \(du = g'(x)\,dx\)
\(\int f(g(x))g'(x)\,dx = \int f(u)\,du\)
Integration by Parts
\(\int u\,dv = uv - \int v\,du\)
Choose u with LIATE: Log, Inverse trig, Algebraic, Trig, Exp
